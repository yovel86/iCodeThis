Features

desktop_windows

Boards

Projects are represented as boards in the system. Along with displaying essential information about the project, they can be set to public or private, depending on user preference

assignment

Tasks

Tasks and subtasks are the smallest chunks of executable assignments. Tasks can be assigned to multiple users, given a priority and easily moved across different states in the development pipeline

groups

Members

Users invited to work together with the common goal of delivering a successful project. They can have several different roles as active members of the. development. or simply followers of the board

timer

Timer

The time tracking feature enables the users to easily measure the time spent on specific task. Once. tracked and logged, the time con be seen and edited in a dedicated Time Records view for additional control

star_border

Follow

Ahead offers the possibility to follow the development of a project and be notified of ts activities. Users can follow entire boards, or they can choose to follow just specific tasks within a board

watch_later

Recently Accessed

The boards that you are actively working on can be easily accessed with the Recently Accessed feature
